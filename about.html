Jose Rizal

Timeline History

The Father of Philippine Independence, a writer, poet, ophthalmologist, polymath and Filipino Nationalist.

1848 - Early Life

The marriage of Jose Rizal's parents, Theodora Morales Alonso-Realonda y Quintos and Francisco Rizal-Mercado Alejandra as Jose Rizal's biological mother and father.

1861 - Birth

June 19th of 1861 was the birth of Jose Rizal, the 7th child of Theodora and Francisco. He was christened Jose Protasio Rizal-Mercado y Alonso-Realonda.

Figure 1a. Jose Rizal's Baptismal Certificate.

1870

Justiniano Aquino Cruz was Jose Rizal's first instructor at age nine.

1872

Jose Rizal passed the entrance exam as a student scholar in St. Tomas University in Manila.

Figure 2a. 11-year-old Jose Rizal.

1896 - Final Days

On December 30th, Jose Rizal was executed by firing squad and secretly buried. He had married Josephine Bracken just two hours prior.

Figure 5a. The execution of Jose Rizal in Bagumbayan (now Rizal Park).

1912 - Legacy

On December 30th, Rizal’s remains were transferred to the Rizal Mausoleum. December 30 is declared a national holiday in his honor.
